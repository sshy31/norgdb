Visualizing Data - Instructions

  • Download desired .bed file containing nuclear organelle DNA data of interest from the Plant Nuclear Organelle DNA Database.
  • Go to Ensembl Plants.
  • In Ensembl Plants, select species of interest to open up additional information.
  • In the genome of assemby section of the species information page, click "Display your data in Ensembl Plants".
  • In the "Data" section of the newly opened tab, click "Choose File" and select the .bed file you downloaded in step 1 from the Plant Nuclear Organelle DNA Database.
  • Once the desired .bed file containing Nuclear Organelle DNA is attached, click add Data.
  • The nuclear organelle DNA data of interest should now be avaible as a custom track viewable on Ensembl Plants.
